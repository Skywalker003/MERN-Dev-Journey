Fluffy Pancakes

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 20 mins

    Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Instructions

Start by mixing all the dry ingredients in a bowl.

Whisk in the eggs and milk until you have a smooth batter.

Cook each side on a medium heat until golden brown.

Tip: Serve with fresh berries and maple syrun for added deliciousness!
